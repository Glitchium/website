About

L00ve is the creative project of Alexander Christian Wulff. Focused on emotional, atmospheric and cinematic music, L00ve blends modern production with deep storytelling to create immersive soundscapes.

Every track is built to feel like a memory, a moment, or a mood you can’t forget.

Vision

To build a world where sound, emotion and identity merge. Not just music — but experience.
